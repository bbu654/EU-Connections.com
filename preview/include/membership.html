How To Join

There is no fee to be an EU member.

To become a member of Experience Unlimited, Anaheim Chapter, you must complete all of the following:

  • Sign Up
    To find out more about Experience Unlimited, call (714)-518-2365, or come into the EU office at 2450 E. Lincoln Ave., Anaheim, CA. Sign up for an Orientation Workshop, held at the EU Anaheim office. Reservations are required, as space is limited. Applications for membership are issued at that time.

  • Attend and complete five workshops
    • Orientation Workshop
    • Resume Writing Workshop
    • Electronic Resume Workshop
    • Interviewing Workshop
    • Networking Workshop

  • Attend an EU Anaheim Committee Meeting
    Experience Unlimited is a volunteer organization. Members volunteer 4 hours per week (16 hours per month) toward EU operations — usually a combination of Committee meeting attendance and office duties. You must choose a Committee as part of your membership. Committee meetings take place bi-weekly or immediately following the completion of the five workshops.

  • Register and post your resume with CalJOBS
    CalJOBS is the California employment database, "California's Internet system for linking employer job listings and job seeker resumes".

After you complete the initial membership requirements, you will have full access to all EU Anaheim resources as long as you maintain active membership status.

Remember there is no fee to join Experience Unlimited.
